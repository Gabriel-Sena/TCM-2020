Catholic Religion

About this religion:

According to the latest IBGE census, 64% of Brazilians are believers in the Catholic religion thus, the religion with the most followers in the Brazilian territory. It was brought to Brazil by Jesuits, at the time of colonization. Catholics believe that Jesus Christ is the incarnation of Himself God and preaches that Jesus died crucified, but was resurrected after three days and ascended to heaven.

Spiritist Religion

About this religion:

According to the last IBGE census, Spiritists represent 2% of the population being the fourth largest Brazilian religion. This religion arose in the attempt of Allan Kardec's attempt to unite philosophy, science and religion. They believe in mediumship (Communication between living and deceased people). For them the figure of Jesus Christ is an inspiration for moral development and the practice of charity.
